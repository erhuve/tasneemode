Dear Neemo,

i saw a red cardinal on the way there, and I actually didn't realize they're common cuz I don't think I ever really saw them before, I also mistakenly thought it was a robin like I think of bluejays or whatever, but I saw it and it told me everything would be okay. and i always know it'll be okay with us but it like spoke to me that it would be okay and I got some comfort from it. it was a vibrant red.

red like your lips that i wanted to see, but i couldnt bear to show my face to you.

red like the cranberry juice you bought for me. of course you would get me juice still.

red like the red on the peculiar red and green honda that just parked on the street. the colors of the bangladeshi flag. and we sat near a banner with bangla written on it. the only word i could read, "amader."

"Our."

red like the scooter of the little boy riding by, and when his older sister fell off her scooter but landed on her feet, he asked "are you okay?" and i smiled and teared up because i just saw ara and ali, and i saw Our future where we and our children play in the sun, and we can think it was all worth it.

and then i thought, it already is.

and i spent the rest of the time trying to muster up something to face you and look you in the eye and tell you something anything to convey it

and the cardinal was right, that it'd be okay, and I'm glad to have found out now that I saw a cardinal not a robin and that cardinals are common, cuz that means it always will be okay if they're common

and i love you

Love, Remi
